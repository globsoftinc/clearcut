Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ClearCut

Is ClearCut really free?

Yes! ClearCut is 100% free with no hidden costs, premium plans, or usage limits. We believe everyone should have access to professional background removal tools.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed! Simply upload your image and start removing backgrounds immediately. No email, no registration, no hassle.

What happens to my images?

Your privacy is our priority. Images are processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. Once processed, they're immediately deleted.

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPG, PNG, and WEBP. Maximum file size is 16MB per image.

How accurate is the background removal?

We use state-of-the-art AI models that provide professional-grade results with precise edge detection, even for complex images with hair and fine details.

Can I use the images commercially?

Absolutely! The processed images are yours to use however you like - personal projects, commercial work, or anything else.
